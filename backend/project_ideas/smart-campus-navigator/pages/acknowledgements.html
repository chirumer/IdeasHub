Smart Campus Navigator: Acknowledgements

Project Overview

Smart Campus Navigator is an innovative AR-powered mobile application designed to revolutionize the way students and visitors navigate university campuses. The app provides real-time directions, detailed building information, and event discovery, promising a seamless and engaging campus experience.
