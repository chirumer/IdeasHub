Smart Campus Navigator

Project Description

Smart Campus Navigator is an AR-powered mobile application designed to revolutionize the way students and visitors explore university campuses. By providing real-time directions, comprehensive building information, and event discovery, it aims to make campus navigation seamless and engaging.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Directions: Using the power of Augmented Reality (AR), the app provides users with real-time, easy-to-follow directions to any location within the campus.
  • Building Information: Users can access detailed information about each building, including its history, departments housed, and working hours.
  • Event Discovery: Stay updated with the latest happenings on campus. The app provides information about upcoming events, workshops, and seminars.

How It Works

The application utilizes AR technology to overlay directional arrows and information bubbles onto the real-world view of the user's device. Simply enter your destination, and the app will guide you there with easy-to-follow visual cues.

Benefits of Smart Campus Navigator

  1. Ease of Navigation: No more getting lost or asking for directions. Navigate the campus like a pro!
  2. Information at Your Fingertips: Get to know your campus better with detailed information about each building and facility.
  3. Never Miss an Event: Stay in the loop with all campus events and activities.
